# About Me
I am currently working as a data scientist for Advent International.

After graduating in 2021, I worked for a year as a data scientist at Boston Consulting Group.
I graduated with my masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin.
Before graduating, I interned at NVIDIA and Salesforce as a data scientist focusing on machine learning applications.
I am passionate about all things engineering, and I enjoy learning about innovations that help improve our lives.
My current focus is to become proficient with techniques in data science and machine learning to do just that.
My hobbies include anything that keeps me physically active, primarily snowboarding, wake surfing, and hiking.
